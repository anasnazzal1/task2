qabatya

qabatya is a city in Jenin Governorate, West Bank, Palestine.
According to the Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics (PCBS) census,
the town had a population of 19,197 in 2007 and 24,439 by 2017
Its entire land area is 50,547 dunams (50.5 km2; 19.5 sq mi),
and its elevation is roughly 256 meters (840 feet).[4] Qabatiya is famous for its olive groves,
modern agriculture, and limestone industry.
